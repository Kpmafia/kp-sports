India take control on Day 2 against Australia

By KP Sports | Match Report

India strengthened their position on Day 2 of the ongoing match against Australia, thanks to a composed batting display and steady support from the middle order.

After resuming the day with confidence, Indian batters focused on building partnerships rather than playing risky shots. This approach helped the team post a competitive total on the scoreboard.

Australia’s bowlers tried to slow the scoring rate, but missed opportunities in the field allowed India to maintain momentum through the sessions.

With the ball, India showed discipline and control, keeping Australian batters under pressure and preventing quick runs.

Why this day matters

Day 2 has clearly tilted the match in India’s favour. A strong first-innings performance gives the bowlers confidence and puts Australia in a position where they must fight hard to stay in the contest.

What to expect next

Australia will look to recover on Day 3 by building partnerships, while India will aim to capitalize early with wickets and tighten their grip on the match.
